About This Listing

This website is a community directory of international academic and research conferences. It is built specifically for students, faculty, and researchers in Bangladesh.

Our Purpose

We provide an organized list of events so you can find submission deadlines and conference dates in one place. This helps you spend less time searching and more time focusing on your research.

Focus Areas

The list includes conferences for Computer Science, Machine Learning, Engineering, and Bioinformatics. We prioritize events with strong international reputations that are relevant to the local research community.

Data Accuracy

We gather dates from official websites. Because deadlines often change, you should always check the official conference link before you submit your work.

Contributions

You can help keep this resource updated. If you know of a missing conference or see an error, please let us know through the Contact page.
