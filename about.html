About This Listing

This site is a curated, community-maintained listing of upcoming international academic and research conferences relevant to researchers, faculty, and students based in Bangladesh.

The goal is simple: provide a clean, easy-to-scan reference so researchers don't have to hunt across multiple sources to track submission deadlines and conference dates.

What's included?

We list conferences across Computer Science, Machine Learning & AI, Human-Computer Interaction, Bioinformatics, and Engineering. The list is not exhaustive — it focuses on conferences with strong international reputations and relevance to the Bangladeshi research community.

Data accuracy

Deadlines and dates are collected from official conference websites. However, dates change frequently. Always verify on the official site before submitting. We note this on every entry.
