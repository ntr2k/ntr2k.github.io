BRAC University celebrates Valentines Day

Publish Date: 
February 14th, 2023

BRAC University organized a live concert on the University’s official (underconstruction) campus on 14 February, 2023 to celebrate Valentines Day. It also coincided with Pohela Falgun, the first day of the 11th month of the Bangla calendar signifying the advent of spring. BRAC University Couples Club (BUCoC) made the arrangements for the live program and the cultural performances.

The program started with a welcome message from Higher Officials, (no) Campus Life, BRAC University. Later, the members of the club presented cultural performances to redefine the meaning of love. 14 songs were performed by the BUCoC members in the event. Some of the songs were - Dur hote tomarey dekhechi and Let me love you. BUCoC members also performed dance on the songs - The Breakup Song and Zara Sa. The event also included colorful decorations prepared by the enthusiastic BUCoC members.
